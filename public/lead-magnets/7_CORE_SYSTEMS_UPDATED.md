### The Problem
- Not all leads are equal (some are ready to buy, others are browsing).
- Agents spend the same time on cold leads as hot leads.
- No visibility into who is most likely to convert.

### What This System Does

**1. Automatic Point Assignment:**
- Opened email: +5 points
- Clicked property link: +10 points
- Visited site 3+ times: +20 points
- Used mortgage calculator: +30 points
- Saved favorites: +25 points

**2. Lead Categorization:**
- Hot (50+ points): Immediate personal follow-up.
- Warm (20-49 points): Automated nurture sequence + weekly check-in.
- Cold (0-19 points): Monthly newsletter only.

**3. Smart Routing:**
- Hot leads ‚Üí Immediate notification to you.
- Warm leads ‚Üí Drip campaign.
- Cold leads ‚Üí Long-term nurture.

### The Integration (How We Wire It Together)
**Lead activity (emails, clicks, visits) ‚Üí Tracked in your CRM ‚Üí Zapier calculates points based on your rules ‚Üí CRM updates lead score automatically ‚Üí Hot leads trigger Slack alert to you + auto-assign to your calendar ‚Üí Warm leads enter nurture sequence ‚Üí Cold leads go to newsletter**

The system watches every interaction and routes accordingly. You focus on the hot ones.

### The Back Office
Want to change the scoring rules? Adjust the point values? Create a new lead category? We do it. Your system adapts without you touching a spreadsheet.

### Typical ROI
- Focus 80% of your time on the 20% of leads most likely to convert.
- 67% increase in conversion rates with lead scoring.
- Never waste time on tire-kickers again.

**Tools Used:** Real Geeks, BoldTrail, Lofty, or custom scoring rules in your CRM.

# Aasani Systems: Periodic Research & Validation Prompt

**Purpose:** Run this prompt quarterly (or before major site updates) to validate claims, refresh statistics, identify new opportunities, and ensure all content remains accurate and compelling.

**Last Updated:** January 2026

---

## How to Use This Prompt

Copy the prompt below and run it with Claude or another AI assistant with web search capabilities. Review the findings and update your site content accordingly.

---

## THE PROMPT

```
You are a research analyst helping Aasani Systems validate and enrich their real estate automation landing page content. Aasani Systems provides done-for-you operational automation for real estate agents — not software tools, but fully built systems that free agents to focus on client relationships.

**Aasani's Core Philosophy:**
"We give agents their time back — so they can be the agent their clients rave about."

The thesis: Clients don't rave about automations. They rave about presence, patience, thoughtfulness, and responsiveness. Automation should enable the human element, not replace it.

**Target Audience:** Real estate agents and small teams who are overwhelmed by admin work and want to focus on relationships and closing deals.

---

## RESEARCH TASKS

Please conduct comprehensive research on the following 10 areas. For each area, provide:
- Current data/statistics with sources
- Any changes from previous benchmarks
- Recommendations for site content updates
- New opportunities or angles discovered

---

### 1. LEAD RESPONSE STATISTICS

Research and verify:
- What percentage of buyers work with the first agent who responds?
- What is the conversion lift for responding in 5 minutes vs 30 minutes?
- What is the average agent response time?
- What percentage of leads are never contacted?
- What percentage of inquiries come after business hours?

Key sources to check: NAR Profile of Home Buyers and Sellers, MIT/InsideSales Lead Response Management Study, Zillow research, Inman surveys.

---

### 2. FOLLOW-UP & PERSISTENCE STATISTICS

Research and verify:
- What percentage of sales require 5+ follow-ups?
- What percentage of salespeople quit after 1-2 follow-ups?
- What is the optimal number of follow-up attempts?
- What are the conversion rates at different follow-up stages?

Key sources to check: Marketing Donut, HubSpot sales research, Scripted, GrowthList, InsideSales.

---

### 3. AGENT TIME ALLOCATION

Research and verify:
- What percentage of agent time is spent on administrative tasks?
- How many hours per week do agents spend on non-revenue activities?
- What percentage of transaction time is administrative work?
- How much time can automation realistically save?

Key sources to check: NAR Member Profile, industry surveys, time management studies, automation vendor research.

---

### 4. TRANSACTION & CLOSING STATISTICS

Research and verify:
- What percentage of transactions close on time?
- What percentage face delays? What causes delays?
- What percentage are terminated/cancelled?
- What is the average time from contract to close?

Key sources to check: NAR, ICE Mortgage Technology, Redfin market reports.

---

### 5. REFERRAL & REPEAT BUSINESS STATISTICS

Research and verify:
- What percentage of agent business comes from referrals?
- What percentage comes from repeat clients?
- How do experienced agents differ from new agents in referral business?
- What drives clients to recommend agents?

Key sources to check: NAR Member Profile, NAR Profile of Home Buyers and Sellers.

---

### 6. CLIENT SATISFACTION & TRUST RESEARCH

Research what clients actually value in agents:
- What factors drive client satisfaction?
- What makes clients recommend agents?
- What language do clients use in positive reviews/testimonials?
- What is the role of responsiveness, availability, and communication?

Key sources to check: NAR satisfaction surveys, Zillow research, real estate review sites, testimonial analysis.

**Specific question:** What do clients rave about? Look for patterns in testimonial language (e.g., "always available," "made me feel," "went above and beyond," "responsive").

---

### 7. EMAIL & COMMUNICATION STATISTICS

Research and verify:
- What is the lift from personalized vs generic emails?
- What are open rates and transaction rates for personalized communication?
- What communication methods do clients prefer?

Key sources to check: Experian Email Market Study, Campaign Monitor, Mailchimp benchmarks.

---

### 8. AI & TECHNOLOGY ADOPTION IN REAL ESTATE

Research current state:
- What percentage of agents use AI tools?
- What percentage see positive impact from AI?
- What are the primary barriers to technology adoption?
- What are agents' fears and objections about automation?

Key sources to check: NAR Technology Survey, Delta Media surveys, industry reports.

---

### 9. COMPETITOR LANDSCAPE

Research current positioning of:
- Follow Up Boss
- kvCORE / BoldTrail
- LionDesk (status check - is it still shutting down?)
- BoomTown
- Top Producer
- Real Geeks
- Wise Agent
- Any new entrants in the "done-for-you" automation space

For each, note:
- Their primary positioning/messaging
- What they sell (software vs services)
- Pricing model if available
- Any messaging that Aasani should avoid (overused) or differentiate from

---

### 10. MARKET CONDITIONS & "WHY NOW" FACTORS

Research current conditions:
- NAR settlement implications and how agents are responding
- Current market conditions (buyer's market, seller's market, interest rates)
- Agent competition levels
- Any regulatory changes affecting agents
- AI advancement and real estate applications
- Any industry disruption or consolidation

---

## OUTPUT FORMAT

Please provide your findings in this structure:

### Executive Summary
- Top 5 most important findings
- Any statistics that have changed significantly
- New opportunities identified
- Recommended content updates

### Detailed Findings by Section
For each of the 10 areas above:
- Current verified statistics with sources
- Comparison to previous data (if known)
- Confidence level (high/medium/low)
- Recommended site copy updates

### New Statistics to Consider Adding
- Any compelling new data points discovered
- Emerging trends worth highlighting
- Fresh angles or proof points

### Content Warnings
- Any claims that could not be verified
- Statistics that appear outdated
- Competitor claims to be aware of

### Source Bibliography
- Full list of sources with URLs
- Publication dates
- Credibility assessment
```
